It’s time to book a fishing trip in Montenegro with Captain Ivan Fishing Tour. Captain Ivan himself will show you a great day out on the water and will guarantee that you’ll catch fish. He is happy to stay longer until you do. Capt. Ivan is a local who speaks English and knows these waters like the back of his hand.
Capt. Ivan is great to fish with as he knows the local history of the area. For example, the live bait you’ll be using is a special type that lives in the muddy parts of the bay. This area used to be an Austro-Hungarian salt factory!
Groups of 10 will have plenty of room on the captain’s 27’ center console boat with a T-top. There are seats in the shade and space to catch some rays in the sun. Three anglers can fish at once.
These short trips are great, as you’ll stay in the calmer waters of the bay. The location and the short trips make Captain Ivan Fishing Tour great for kids. Throughout the year you’ll be able to catch the likes of Common Pandora, Mahi Mahi, Sea Bream, Mackerel, and Bonito. These are great tablefare that can be cooked for you by local restaurants for a small charge.
